Boil in the Bag: Multiple Regression (3 Predictors)

Three predictors, one outcome

Author

Gordon Wright

Modified

Overview

Use this template when: You want to predict a continuous outcome from three continuous (or categorical) predictors.

Example scenarios: - Predicting academic performance from motivation, ability, AND study time - Predicting wellbeing from sleep, exercise, AND social connection - Predicting lie detection accuracy from empathy, experience, AND anxiety
